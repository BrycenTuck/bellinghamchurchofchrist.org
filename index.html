Our Mission

As disciples of Jesus, we strive to live out the gospel and to share it with others. Whether in worship on Sundays, in Small Group gatherings, or in one-on-one Bible studies, Gods word needs to be known and to be made known. It is because Jesus gave His life for us that we have a passion to share the good news with you!
